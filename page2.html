Types of
Skiing











There are many types of skiing, but there are 4 main categories of skiing that are
the most recognizable.

  • Nordic
  • Alpine
  • Telemark
  • Competition

Nordic skiing is marked by a difference in skiing boot, and is mostly comprised of
cross-country and skijumping disciplines. Nordic skiers have a unique boot that
doesn't attach at the heel, meaning skiers can lift most of their foot of the
ski without risk. Nordic skiing can be practiced in backcountry or on marked
groomed trails. Alpine skiing, also known as downhill skiing, was created around
the same time as ski lifts, because skiers no longer had to hike or ski uphill
like in Nordic skiing.
