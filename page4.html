Alpine
Skiing











Alpine skiing, also known as downhill skiing, was created around
the same time as ski lifts, because skiers no longer had to hike or ski uphill
like in Nordic skiing. Alpine skiing was origninally very similar to Nordic
skiing because it split off of it, but has since change significantly. Some of
the most notable differences between Nordic and Alpine is how the Alpine
skiing boot is shaped, with an extra hook around the heel and significantly
different techniques.
