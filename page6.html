Skiing











Skiing has a long history with competitions and challenges. Skiing has many
different forms it can be seen in. A few of these disciplines include
ski jumping, cross-country skiing, slalom, and speed skiing. Olympically,
Nordic and Alpine skiing have both been events since 1924 and 1936
respectively, however Telemark has recently been denied as an event in the
2022 Beijing Games.
