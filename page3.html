Nordic
Skiing











Nordic skiing is marked by a difference in skiing boot, and is mostly comprised of
cross-country and skijumping disciplines. Nordic skiers have a unique boot that
doesn't attach at the heel, meaning skiers can lift most of their foot of the
ski without risk. Nordic skiing can be practiced in backcountry or on marked
groomed trails.
