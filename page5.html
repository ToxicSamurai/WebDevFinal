Telemark
Skiing











Telemark is a technique that originated from Telemark, Norway, and is one
of the most similar techniques to Nordic skiing. Telemark skiing often
uses highly specialized equipment, such as ski skins to help uphill
skiing and skis that only attach to the toe of the ski boot. Telemark
skiing can often be distinguished by its unique turning style, which
looks almost like lunging.
